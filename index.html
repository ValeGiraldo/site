How to be a good internet user

  • Digital etiquette
  • Verify information
  • Protect privacy
  • Positive community

Practice Digital Etiquette

Treat others online with respect and kindness, just as you would in face-to-face interactions. Avoid engaging in cyberbullying, spreading rumors, or harassing others. Remember that there are real people behind every screen, and your words and actions can have a significant impact.

Verify Information Before Sharing

With the abundance of information available online, it's essential to verify the accuracy of what you share before spreading it further. Check the credibility of sources, look for corroborating evidence, and be cautious of misinformation or fake news. Sharing accurate information helps maintain the integrity of online discourse and prevents the spread of falsehoods.

Protect your Privacy and Security

Safeguard your personal information and online accounts by using strong, unique passwords, enabling two-factor authentication whenever possible, and being cautious about the information you share publicly. Be wary of phishing attempts and scams, and regularly update your devices and software to protect against security vulnerabilities.

Promote positive online Communities

Contribute to creating a positive and inclusive online environment by participating in constructive discussions, supporting others, and standing up against online harassment or negativity. Encourage empathy, understanding, and open-mindedness in your interactions, and strive to build connections based on mutual respect and shared interests.
